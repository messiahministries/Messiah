About Messiah Ministry

Messiah Ministry Church is dedicated to spreading the love of Christ and serving the people of Madurai. Our vision is to bring hope, healing, and transformation through the gospel. We welcome everyone to join us in worship, prayer, and community service.

Mission: To share the gospel and build a Christ-centered community.
